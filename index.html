Marucs Freeman

Marcus freeman is the head coach of the Notre Dame football team. He is now entering his 4th year as the head coach. Freeman was hired after the previous head coach, Brian Kelly, left the team to "go to team with the capabilities to win the national championship." Freeman instead turned Notre Dame into a national championship contender by changing the culture for the fighting irish.

Mike Denbrock

Mike Denbrock is the offensive cordinator for the Notre Dame Fight Irish. Denbrock has been with Notre Dame 3 seperate times in his coaching career. His current tenure as offensive cordinator started in 2023. He has since brought a dynamic offense that can put points on the board. His offenses typically come in the form of a dominant run game.

Al Golden

Al Golden is the defensive cordinator for the Notre Dame Fighting Irish. Bringing in creative and agressive play calls, he transformed the defense into one of the top in the nation. Golden has been at Notre dame as the defensive cordinator for 3 seasons. His work has been impressive enough to gain the attention of the NFL. Golden will be taking his talents to Cincinatti next season to be the defensive cordinator for the Bengals.
